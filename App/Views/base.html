About PiBake

PiBake is a modular, cloud-based, sensor network solution for enterprise data gathering solutions across North America and the world. We use a series of specialized digital sensor tools integrated with our custom designed Raspberry Pi hardware to deliver data to our proprietary web interface for clients on any device.

At Team PiBake, we strive to use technology to solve data problems in a way that's accessible and elegant. We are firm believers in ability to use web distributed tech to help our clients, but we know they deserve to interact with real human beings. Each PiBake cluster is overseen and administered by expert members of our staff who know our cutting-edge processes and love the people we serve.
